    The cost of the trip is paid by the passenger with a linked bank card, that is, there are no risks associated with carrying cash for both the passenger and the driver, and you don't need to look for small change for exchange.

    We have a minimum commission and we pay bonuses for each trip, while some of our competitors keep the bonuses for themselves. We work honestly and drivers earn more with us!

    We pay salaries to the driver's personal bank card! You don't need to register an LLC, individual entrepreneur, pay taxes, file a declaration, pay accounting services, and maintain a bank account.

    You simply click the button to go online to start working, and when you decide to finish, click to go offline. You can work only in the morning, evening, on weekends or holidays!
